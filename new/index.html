What is Farm Aid ?

Farm Aid is a technology solution that adopts pull and push system using short message service (SMS), Interactive Voice Response (IVR), Web and farmer call center to engage and provide information on good agricultural practices(GAP) as it relates to crop production, market prices of crops, weather information, business advisory, market linkage, farm inputs sourcing, livestock production and animal health to smallholder farmers. As well as providing a channel which farmers can ask questions on the different aspects of their processes.

Services & Features

Farm Aid is an integrated Agriculture extension portal that has a call centre of agricultural experts where you can send in your questions via SMS or make a call to the farmer call center to speak to an expert for instant help on agri-related challenges. Once you sign up, you will also receive agri tips on crop and livestock, market prices and weather updates.

Good Agricultural Practices (GAP)

Instant SMS on farming best practice. For market actors, deliver your message to Farm Aid’s large database of profiled farmers, to build your products’ brand awareness and increase sales..

Weather Advisory Services

Accurate weather information sourced from proven and reliable weather service provider to help smallholder farmer plan their processes.

Animal Health & Livestock Production

Paravets and Community Animal health workers (CAHW) can subscribe to access information on the diagnosis, treatment and vaccines for animal diseases and sicknesses.

Business Advisory & financial literacy

Provision of business advisory and financial tips on crop budgeting and farm management in order to understand the economics of production before accessing loans and other input credit.

Access to Quality Inputs

Access to genuine/improved crop inputs from trusted input suppliers and seed companies

Market Linkage & Commodity Pricing

Get updated information on the prices of food crops to inform your decision on where to sell and when to sell, also get access to information on the demands of premium buyers.
